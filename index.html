Our advisors

Elise Brett, MD

Advisor

Dr. Elise Brett is an Associate Clinical Professor - Medicine, Endocrinology, Diabetes and Bone Disease at The Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and faculty at Lenox Hill Hospital. She received her B.S. from the University of Michigan and her M.D. degree from Mount Sinai School of Medicine. Dr. Brett is Endocrine Certified in Neck Ultrasound and is a Certified Nutrition Support Clinician. She is a member of the American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists (AACE), the American Society for Parenteral and Enteral Nutrition (ASPEN), the American Diabetes Association and The Endocrine Society. Dr. Brett with Dr. Jeffrey Mechanick has co-edited and published various books including, Nutritional Strategies for the Diabetic and Prediabetic Patient, The Complete Guide to Lifelong Nutrition, and two practical handbooks - Nutritional Medicine Pocket and Diabetes Pocket books.

William Reisacher, MD

Advisor

Dr. Reisacher is an Associate Professor of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery and Director of Allergy Services at Weill Cornell Medical College/NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital in NYC. He graduated from Cornell University in 1990 and graduated with Distinction in Research from The Mount Sinai School of Medicine in 1994. Dr. Reisacher is a Fellow of the American Academy of Otolaryngic Allergy (AAOA), where he has also served on the Board of Directors. He has been a co-founder, inventor and senior advisor for several early-stage companies in the biotechnology and digital health-tech spaces.
